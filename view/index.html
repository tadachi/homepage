About Me


I am a developer in Victoria, BC. Most of my projects are web based, But I can build a variety of web applications that fit your needs. My strong points include HTML/CSS, Javascript(JQuery,AngularJS), and NodeJS.

As of recently (end of 2014-2015), I've dived into Android Development head first. And am pursuing a career in it.

I'm not limited to just developing. I have also a strong background in Information Technology. I regularly troubleshoot networks, computers, and handhelds. You can count on me to quickly catch up to your IT practices.

My favorite web hangouts include Hacker News, Reddit, Github, and Twitch. Kendo is my main physical activity and cycling is my main mode of exercise and transportation besides driving.

Most recent employment was with Applocation as an Android Contract Developer. I had a wonderful time there. It was my first professional programming experience and I wouldn't trade it for anything in the world.
